Core Objective

Create an elegant online presence for MHRInteriors.com that showcases the quality and experience of Marian's work to create spaces of joy, peace, and comfort.

Project Scope & Deliverables

A simple, clean website designed around your projects. You'll be able to add, edit, and remove projects and photos yourself from an easy-to-use editing portal—no developer needed.
  • Full website design—thoughtful, minimal design that works on all screen sizes including the following pages:
    Home / Projects Symmetrical grid of project thumbnails—this is the landing page visitors see first
    Project Detail Title, short description, and full photo gallery for each project
    About Your portrait photo, bio, and design statement
    Contact Simple contact form, submissions sent directly to your email
  • Content setup—your photos, project descriptions, and other content added to the site for you
  • Editing portal—add, edit, or remove projects and photos yourself
  • SEO—optimized for Google search and social sharing previews (Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn)
  • Deployment—live on mhrinteriors.com

Process & Timeline

Once the proposal is approved and initial payment is received:
  • Site creation begins—I'll begin creating the site, incorporating the content you provide.
  • Review link—Within 7 days, I'll send you a link to review.
  • Feedback—You'll compile your feedback as a list of specific changes—e.g. layout, typography, colors, image sizing, etc.
  • Review call—We'll meet for a 30-minute review call to walk through your feedback and clarify any details.
  • Publish—I'll apply those changes, conduct a final checklist review of the site, and publish.
  • Editing walkthrough—I'll deliver a recorded walkthrough showing you how to manage all the site content independently.
* Additional revision rounds are billed at $175/hour.
